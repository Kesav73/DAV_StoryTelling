Sizing Secrets: Decode Your Fit

Exploring the relationship between Shirt Size, Shoe Size, and Gender

Introduction

Does Shirt Size correlate with Shoe Size? Are there gender-based differences? Let's dive into the data to uncover insights.

The Data Behind the Story

We collected data on Shirt Size, Shoe Size, and Gender from 99 participants to analyze patterns.

Exploratory Data Analysis

Shirt Size Distribution

Most participants wear M and L size shirts, with fewer in XS and 3XL sizes.

Shoe Size Distribution

Shoe sizes range from 4 to 12, with the majority around sizes 8-10.

Shoe Size by Gender

Males generally have a wider range of shoe sizes, whereas females mostly fall within a smaller range.

Cluster Analysis

We applied K-Means clustering to identify natural groupings in the data.

The Elbow Method suggests that 3 clusters are optimal for segmenting our data.

Key Insights

  • Larger Shirt Sizes tend to have larger Shoe Sizes showing a linear relation.
  • Males show more variation in Shoe Size compared to Females.
  • Females have relatively smaller Shoe Sizes.
